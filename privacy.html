Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how VultSight ("VultSight", "we", "us") handles personal information collected through this website. It applies to this website only. Our products and managed services are governed by the separate agreements in place with each customer.

Information we collect

We collect only what we need to respond to you and operate the site:

  • Information you provide — such as your name, work email, company and any message you submit through our contact form.
  • Information collected automatically — basic technical data such as your browser type, device, approximate location and pages viewed, gathered through standard logs and analytics.

How we use information

  • To respond to enquiries and provide the information you request.
  • To operate, maintain and improve the website.
  • To communicate with you about VultSight where you have asked us to, and to keep records of those communications.
  • To protect the security and integrity of the site and comply with applicable law.

We do not sell your personal information.

Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies and similar technologies to keep the site functioning and to understand, in aggregate, how it is used. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling them may affect some functionality.

Sharing

We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate the website (for example, hosting or analytics providers), under appropriate confidentiality and data-protection obligations. We may also disclose information where required by law or to protect our rights. We do not share your information with third parties for their own marketing.

Data retention

We keep personal information only for as long as needed for the purposes described above, or as required by law, and then delete or anonymise it.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete or restrict the use of your personal information, or to object to certain processing. To make a request, contact us using the details below. We will respond in line with applicable law.

International transfers

VultSight operates internationally, and your information may be processed in countries other than your own. Where it is, we take steps to ensure it is treated consistently with this policy and applicable law.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures designed to protect personal information. See our Security overview for more.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date above. Please review it periodically.
