How it works

From discovery to resolution

Discover → Poll → Evaluate → Alert → Resolve. A continuous monitoring loop with auto-discovery and threshold-driven alerting.

🧭

1 · Discover

Auto-discovery rules sweep your network and surface new devices for approval — nothing monitored without intent.

📶

2 · Poll

Scheduled SNMP, ICMP/ping and TCP/HTTP checks collect availability and performance from every device and service.

📊

3 · Evaluate

Metrics are measured against thresholds and SLA targets — CPU, memory, latency, packet loss, interface and bandwidth.

🚨

4 · Alert

The NOC health worker raises alerts on breaches and outages, with maintenance windows suppressing planned work.

✅

5 · Resolve

Alerts roll up into NOC incidents with MTTR tracking, then close out into uptime and SLA-compliance reporting.

🖥️
